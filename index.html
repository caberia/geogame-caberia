Welcome to the "Finding US States" game

The game is pretty straightforward:


- I give you a blank map of US states and want you to find and click to a specific state.

- You have 1.5 Minutes to find as many states as possible.

- If you click the wrong state, you don't lose lives, but you lose precious time!

- Try to get the highest score before the time runs out.


Good luck.


Note: The game also includes the territories of the United States alongside the states.
